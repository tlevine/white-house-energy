WHITEHOUSE / YOUR HOUSE

APP FOR ENERGY EDUCATION

Use This App To Help Students Gain Energy Literacy. The Concept Is Simple, Using An Iconic Building Known To All Students, We Are Able To Drive Interest In Learning About Energy Related Issues. Once You Have Garnered Interest You Can Then Investigate Your Own Building/Home In Comparison To The White House.

  • Iconic—well known
  • Interesting Connections
  • How Does Presidents Travel Impact Energy Consumption At The White House?
  • How Do Visits By Dignitaries Affect Energy Usage At The White House?
  • How Do The Seasons Affect Energy Usage At The White House
  • You Can Then Go One Step Deeper And Compare Other Iconic Buildings.

By Arsalan Danish, Thomas Levine, and Joshua Sneideman
